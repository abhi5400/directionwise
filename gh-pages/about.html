About Direction Wise Tourism

Who We Are

Direction Wise Tourism LLC is a premier travel agency based in Dubai, UAE, specializing in luxury tours and VIP travel experiences. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating unforgettable memories for our guests in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class travel experiences that showcase the beauty, culture, and luxury of the United Arab Emirates. We strive to exceed expectations through personalized service, expert guidance, and attention to every detail.

Our Vision

To be the leading choice for luxury travel experiences in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, recognized for our commitment to excellence, cultural authenticity, and exceptional customer service.

Why Choose Us

  • Licensed Expertise: Our founder is a licensed UAE tour guide with over 15 years of experience
  • Premium Service: We specialize in luxury tours and VIP experiences
  • Personalized Attention: Every tour is customized to meet your preferences
  • Local Knowledge: Deep understanding of Dubai and Abu Dhabi's culture and history
  • Comprehensive Services: From city tours to desert safaris, hotel bookings to custom itineraries

Our Founder

Niranjan Singh Shekhawat

Licensed UAE Tour Guide | 15+ Years Experience

With over 15 years of experience as a licensed tour guide in the UAE, Niranjan Singh Shekhawat founded Direction Wise Tourism to share his passion for the region's rich culture, stunning architecture, and breathtaking landscapes.

His expertise and dedication ensure that every guest receives an authentic, memorable, and luxurious travel experience in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.
